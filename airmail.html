AIRMAIL — OLD-SCHOOL FLYING

Deliver letters and parcels between sleepy little airfields.

Controls (keyboard):
W/S pitch down/up   A/D roll   Q/E yaw
Shift or ] throttle up   [ throttle down (and brakes at idle)
Space transmit on the radio   ? help   Esc pause

Touch: left stick = pitch/roll. Tap the brass radio dial to call ahead for a heading.

Delivering: Fly to your destination airfield, land on the runway, and come to a full stop. Mail is then dispatched and a fresh parcel drops in.

Clock: tap the clock to enlarge it. In the enlarged view, tap the centre to return to local wallclock time, tap the middle ring for AM hours, or tap the outer ring for PM hours.

No bearings here, just compass directions. The miniature world is procedurally generated from a fixed seed, so airfields stay in the same places every flight.

PAUSED

Press Esc to resume, or review the controls before taking off again.
