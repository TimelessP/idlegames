ABOUT

Deliver letters and parcels between sleepy little airfields in a tiny procedural countryside.

No bearings here, just compass directions. The world is generated from a fixed seed, so the same strips and landmarks remain in place every flight.

Land on the runway at your destination, come to a full stop, and the post is dispatched before a fresh envelope drops aboard.
