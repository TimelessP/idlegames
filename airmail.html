On Foot: use W/S to walk forward and backward, A/D to turn, or the left touch pad to move and steer. Tap the plane or press Enter within 3m to board. Pilots can swim.

Touch: left stick = pitch/roll, right stick = throttle with rudder on the horizontal axis. Hold either throttle control at idle on the ground to apply brakes.

Instruments: tap the clock or compass to enlarge them. In the enlarged clock, tap the centre to restore local time, the middle ring for AM, and the outer ring for PM.

ABOUT

Deliver letters and parcels between sleepy little airfields in a tiny procedural countryside.

No bearings here, just compass directions. The world is generated from a fixed seed, so the same strips and landmarks remain in place every flight.

Land on the runway at your destination, come to a full stop, and the post is dispatched before a fresh envelope drops aboard.
