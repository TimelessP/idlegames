🐂 How to Play

Lead your ox wagon across the frontier! Manage resources, trade in towns, and survive the journey.

Controls:

  • A/D or ◀/▶ — Walk (on foot) or drive wagon (when mounted)
  • W — Dismount wagon
  • E / Enter / Menu button — Open action menu

Action Menu:

  • Press E or tap Menu to open
  • Nearby actions appear at the top (pick up, chop, fish, trade, guestbook, build/rest at home…)
  • Manage section: Wagon Inventory, Ox Management, Make Camp, Status
  • Navigate with Tab, arrows, W/S; confirm with E/Enter; close with Esc

Wagon:

  • Walk near wagon → Menu → Board Wagon
  • Tap A/D to start/stop driving
  • 8 base inventory slots; install a canopy to unlock +8 canopy slots (16 total)
  • Speed depends on number of hitched oxen
  • Auto-converts to raft at river crossings
  • If wheel breaks: repair with a spare wheel, or with 2 wood near the wagon

Survival:

  • Manage hunger, thirst, and health
  • Chop wood → Make Camp → Sleep until dawn
  • Chopping a mature tree yields 3 wood + 1 seed, with a 1 in 8 chance of 2 seeds; seeds can be eaten, sold, planted, or fed
  • Planted seeds grow into trees over time; only fully grown trees can be chopped
  • Build cornfields with 1 Water Butt + 2 wood, water while growing, then reap for corn
  • Fish at rivers (bank or raft): 10% wheel, 10% wood, 60% fish, 20% no catch
  • Caught/chopped resources auto-stow in wagon when space is available
  • Oxen need water and grazing — unleash at rivers
  • Trade goods in towns for gold
  • Each town has a hotel; stay overnight for 2 gold to wake healed, fed, watered, and ready for morning
  • With 6 wood (on foot, on land), build a permanent house; rest there to fully recover all and reset to morning + clear weather
  • Any nearby house or hotel has a unique Guestbook note page that auto-saves as you type

Progress Save:

  • Your journey autosaves to browser storage while you play
  • Close the browser anytime and continue later from where you left off
  • New Game starts fresh and clears previous run progress
